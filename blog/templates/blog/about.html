Hi to everyone👋

Welcome to the DIMOSPHEN1/ blog😊

Below you can find several tips regarding its functionality. Feel free to explore this site. Maybe, you can even create an account and post something here😉. Enjoy!

To create a profile, you will need to make the following steps.

Step 1. Click on the REGISTER button on the top right corner of the page.

Step 2. Enter required data and after that click on Sign Up button.

Step 3. After that you are signed up on the site! Congratulations👏

Step 4. Currently your profile image is set to the default one with green background. But you can change that.

Step 5. Just upload new image and it will be set as your new one. You can update your profile information here as well😊

To make a post, you will need to make the following steps.

Step 1. Click on the NEW POST button on the top right corner of the page.

Step 2. Type the title, content of the post and after that click on Post button.

Step 3. You will be redirected to the page with your post! Congratulations, it's now on the site👏 Here you can update or delete your post as well.

Step 4. You can see all posts that are currently on the site by pressing HOME button. You should see your post there.

Step 5. Pay attention, you can update or delete post that are authored by you, not by other users👆

To fluently navigate through this site, you will need to make the following steps.

Step 1. Scroll down to the bottom of the page. You will see several buttons there.

Step 2. You can navigate either by pressing numbers or Next/Last | Previous/First buttons.

Step 3. When you will rich the end, there will be only an option to navigate backwards. Congratulations, now you can see all posts on this site👏

To chose post of certain author, you will need to make the following steps.

Step 1. Click on the author's username in one of the posts. It will change color from green to black😉

Step 2. That's all! Now you see only posts made by certain author, as well as their number next to the author's username.

Step 3. Navigation through pages remains the same as it was on the main page.

To reset a password, you will need to make the following steps.

Step 1. If you are logged in, click on the LOGOUT button on the top right corner of the page. Otherwise, miss this step.

Step 2. After that, you need to reach the login page by either pressing Log In Again button or clicking LOGIN on the top right corner of the page.

Step 3. You will be redirected to the Log In page where you need to click on Forgot Password. It is underlined with the blue line.

Step 4. On the Reset Password page, you need to type an e-mail that you entered during your first registration. After that press the Request Password Reset button.

Step 5. Now you will receive an e-mail with further instructions from dimosphen1.blog@gmail.com. Pay attention that this e-mail can be automatically sent to the Spam folder in your e-mail box. Be sure that the e-mail came exactly from dimosphen1.blog@gmail.com. Follow instructions in an e-mail and reset your password. Congratulations, you have done it👏
